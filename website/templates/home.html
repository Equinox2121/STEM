STEM Community Service

Participants: The community service requirement for this academic program is designed to encourage character development, civic engagement, and practical, real-world experience in STEM-related fields. The service component supports program and classroom goals by involving students directly in their local STEM communities. Students who participate are expected to document their service through a service log and a written activity report describing their contributions. Community service is defined as a voluntary contribution of time and/or effort to support school or community members and may be completed during the academic year, summer break, or holiday periods. Students may fulfill this requirement through a variety of opportunities, including family-supported volunteering, youth organizations, or independent service activities. All service experiences should have a clear connection to STEM.

Students may begin accumulating service hours upon entry into the program. All required service must be completed by a specified deadline during the student’s graduating year. Students are responsible for ensuring that each activity includes a clear application of STEM principles and receives approval from an appropriate instructor or program supervisor.
